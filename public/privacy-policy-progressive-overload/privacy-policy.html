Privacy Policy - Progressive Overload

Effective date: February 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Progressive Overload (Android package: com.anonymous.progressiveoverload).

1. Who we are

Developer: Progressive Overload

Privacy contact: michael0745@protonmail.com

2. Data handling summary

Progressive Overload is designed as a privacy-first workout logger.

  • No ad SDKs are used.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are used.
  • No account system or cloud backend is used.
  • Workout data is stored locally on your device.

3. What data the app stores

The app stores workout-related data locally on the device, including:

  • Profiles and settings (language, timezone, weight unit)
  • Workouts and exercises
  • Session logs (sets, reps, weights, timestamps, notes, timers)
  • History and statistics generated from your entries

This data is stored using on-device app storage (AsyncStorage).

4. Data collection and sharing

By default, the app does not automatically collect, upload, transmit, or sell your personal data.

Data leaves your device only when you explicitly choose export/share actions, such as:

  • Exporting JSON/CSV
  • Sharing files via the system share sheet
  • Sending CSV by email through your email app

When you share data using other apps (for example, email or cloud drives), those third-party services process data under their own privacy policies.

5. Permissions

The app is configured to minimize permissions and does not request unnecessary high-risk permissions such as storage read/write or system overlay access.

6. Data retention and deletion

Your workout data remains on your device until you delete it.

You can remove data by:

  • Deleting profiles/workouts/sessions inside the app
  • Clearing app data in Android settings
  • Uninstalling the app

7. Security

Data is stored locally on device storage. While reasonable care is taken in app design, no storage method is completely risk-free.

If your device is compromised, local data may be exposed.

8. Children

This app is not specifically directed to children under 13.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version is indicated by the effective date at the top of this page.
